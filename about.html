Why Will Daycare & Preschool?

WDP provides an empowering learning environment where all members of the community are
challenged to achieve their potential, encouraged to pursue their passions, and expected to fulfil
their responsibilities. Numerous programmes support students and parents through WDP journey.

Classrooms that Inspire Learning.

“Early childhood classrooms are special places; within them discoveries abound, imaginations soar, and anything is possible.

At Will-Daycare & Preschool we believe that environment is the key to creating a joyful place for childhood; and we put a great deal of thought and care into creating those joyful places. In keeping with Reggio Emilia and Montessori philosophies, our designs consider not only what children need in a space, but how they will use it. We know our centers will form an integral part of a child’s earliest learning; and with that in mind, we carefully curate high-quality materials for every center to create beautiful environments with rich, open-ended experiences that nurture development and inspire learning.
